Who we are

We are a registered charity in Nigeria named One step.We are one, global community and duty bound to play our part in humanitarian causes across the world.

What we do

The aim is to get all kids off the streets. We also support orphans and impoverished communities by facilitating their education for a brighter future. We believe this initiative will help them to stand on their own feet and inspire others to do likewise and thereby, creating a successful chain of people willing to extend their knowledge and expertise and overcoming the cycle of poverty and inequalities that currently exists.
